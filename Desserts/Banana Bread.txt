Banana Bread
1 loaf

INGREDIENTS

* 3-4 smashed bananas
* 1/3 cups melted butter
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 tsp. baking soda
* 1 egg
* 1 cup whole wheat flour
* 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
* 1 tsp. vanilla extract
* pinch of salt

METHOD

* Mix melted butter and bananas.
* Add sugar, egg and vanilla.
* Add salt and baking soda.
* Add flour.
* Bake at 350dF for 1 hour.
