Chocolate Chip Cookies
Makes 24 cookies

INGREDIENTS

* 1 cup butter
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 2 eggs
* 2 tsp. vanilla extract
* 3 cups flour
* 1 tsp. baking soda
* 2 tsp. water
* 2 cups chocolate chips

METHOD

* Cream butter and sugar.
* Add eggs and vanilla and combine.
* Add baking soda and water.
* Gradually add flour until incorporated.
* Add chocolate chips.
* Divide into cookies on an cookie sheet.
* Bake in 350dF oven for 10-20min.
