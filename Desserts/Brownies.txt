Brownies
Serves 6

EQUIPMENT
* 1 8x8 inch baking pan, buttered and floured

INGREDIENTS

* 1/2 cup butter, melted and cooled 
* 1 cup granulated sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1/3 cup cocoa powder
* 1 tsp. vanilla extract
* 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
* 1/4 tsp. baking powder

METHOD

* Combine sugar, eggs and vanilla in a large bowl; add melted butter to sugar mixture and stir to combine.
* In a separate bowl combine the cocoa powder, flour, salt and baking powder then gradually add to the wet ingredients.
* Place batter into a baking pan and bake at 350dF for 25-30 minutes.
