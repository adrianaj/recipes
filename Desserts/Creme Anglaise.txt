Serves 4

INGREDIENTS

* 1 cup milk
* 1 cup whipping cream
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1/4 vanilla bean
* 4 egg yolks

METHOD

* Add milk, cream, vanilla and 1/4 cup sugar to a pan.
* Bring to boil.
* Whisk eggs and 1/4 cup sugar in a bowl.
* Whisk in milk mixture gradually.
* Return to pan and stir until thick.
* Refrigerate
