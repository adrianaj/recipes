Pizza Dough
For 2 personal pizzas

INGREDIENTS

* 1/2 tsp. sugar
* 1/2 cup warm water
* 1 tsp. yeast
* 1 tsp. olive oil
* 1 tsp. salt
* 2/3 cup whole wheat flour
* 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

METHOD

* Dissolve sugar in water, add yeast and let stand for 10min.
* Stir in olive oil and salt.
* Add flour.
* Put dough on a flat surface and knead for 10min.
* Place in oiled bowl and cover dough with oil.
* Cover bowl with towel and let stand for approx. 1hr.
* Cut when risen. Form two tight balls.
* Let rise for 45min.
* Bake in 425dF oven for 16-20min.
