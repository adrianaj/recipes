Martin's Curry Tuna Cream Cheese Pasta
Serves 2

INGREDIENTS

* 1 tub cream cheese
* 2 tins tuna
* some vegetables
* a bunch of pasta
* some curry powder
* salt

METHOD

* Place pasta, vegetables, curry powder, and tuna and salt into a pot of water, bring to a boil, and boil until pasta is done.
* Drain water and return to pot.
* Add more curry powder to taste.
* Add chunks of cream cheese and stir over low heat until combined.
