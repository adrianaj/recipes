Dijon Pork Chops
Serves 2


INGREDIENTS

* 2 thick cut pork chops
* 1/2 tbsp. butter
* 1/2 tbsp olive oil
* 1/8 cup shallots
* 1/2 cup white wine
* 1/3 cup chicken stock
* 1/3 cup heavy cream
* 1 tbsp. Dijon Mustard

METHOD

* In a pan, heat olive oil and butter together.  Once hot, sear the pork chops for 2 minutes on each side then remove to a plate.
* Add shallots to the pan and cook for 1 minute, then add 1/4 cup of the wine and bring to a boil.
* Once the wine boils turn d the heat, add the stock, return the pork chops to the pan, and cook for 10 minutes turning the pork chops once. 
* After 10 minutes, remove pork chops to plate, add the second 1/4 cup of wine to the pan, bring the liquid to a boil to reduce for 3 minutes.
* After the liquid reduces, add the cream and boil to reduce for another 3 minutes.  
* Once sauce is reduced, stir in dijon mustard and serve with pork chops.
